The Prospera SEED round is an initial RWA start up investment zone where early backers can purchase Prospera tokens (PROP) at a privileged rate on pilot mode. This round is designed to raise funds to support the development and expansion of our real estate tokenization platform, allowing investors to be part of our journey from the ground up.

To participate in the SEED round:
  1. First you need to Login through our website www.prosperabuild.com
  2. Then click on participate now button.
  3. After that connect your WEB 3 wallet by clicking on connect button.
  4. A pop should ask you to register your wallet, accept and confirm.
  5. Enter the amount you wish to participate with, the prompt should show you the USDT amount you need to pay.
  6. To confirm your participation click on buy now button and the amount will be deducted from your wallet automatically.

Investing in the SEED round offers several benefits:
  • Privileged Rates: Purchase tokens at a lower price before they are publicly available.
  • Early Access: Be among the first to invest in Prosperas innovative RWA ecosystem.
  • Exclusive Rewards: Early investors of the SEED round can enjoy exclusive refer & earn rewards.

Prospera uses block chain technology and secure smart contracts to ensure the security and transparency of all transactions. Our platform is built with the latest security protocols to protect your investment. Additionally, all properties are carefully vetted and legally compliant, providing an extra layer of security for your investment.
Also traditionally real estate brings its security measures to the ecosystem. Prospera has a global reach of real estate assets from Romania, Bulgaria, UK, USA, Canada, Dubai and much more. This brings the ecosystem a boost and empowers its community members with seamless asset investments.
Prospera ecosystem is being audited by the worlds no 1 company, where the KYC process of the project is also done by the same.

To swap any token into a BEP-20 token, visit our website, scroll to the second section, and click on the SWAP button. We also have a detailed educational video on our social media channels to guide you through this process.

Our Refer and Earn program is exclusively available for the SEED round, and you must be a participant to be eligible for refer & earn:
  • Step 1: On the same screen where you participated in the seed round, you can see a refer & earn section, click on the copy link button.
  • Step 2: Now you can share the link with the person you wish to refer.
You shall receive the following rewards for each referral:
  • At Level 1: Get up to a 4% bonus for each successful referral.
  • At Level 2: Get up to a 3% bonus for each successful referral made by your referrals.
  • At Level 3: Get up to a 1% bonus for each successful referral made by the referrals of your referrals.
